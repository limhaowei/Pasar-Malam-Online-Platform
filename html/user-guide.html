User Guide

1. What information are required for registering a vendor account?

  • Name of the business
  • Email address
  • Phone Number
  • SSM Number (Company Registration Number)
  • Link to Social Media (e.g. Instagram / Facebook)
  • Product Name
  • Product Type (Choose from "F&B" and "Non-F&B")
  • Typhoid Certificate (For F&B vendors only)
  • Product Picture
  • Product Menu / List

2. How do I apply for a slot in the Lorong Belakang Night Market?

  1. Register as a vendor
  2. Log in to your account
  3. Apply for a slot
  4. Fill in the required information
  5. Submit your application
  6. Wait for the result
  7. Upon shortlisted, you will receive a link for uploading payment proof
  8. After verifying the payment, you will receive information regarding your allocated booth, including the booth number
